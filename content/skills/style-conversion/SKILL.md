# Style Conversion

## Runtime Label

Conversion-Focused Style Guide

## Style Rules

- Focus on user pain, desired outcome, and decision clarity.
- Emphasize concrete benefits, proof, and next action.
- Keep copy specific and outcome-oriented.
- Minimize abstraction; prioritize action-driving phrasing.
- Avoid manipulative language and unsupported claims.

## Structure Guidance

- Lead with the primary pain or opportunity.
- Present value proposition and proof.
- End with one clear CTA and minimal friction.
