# Channel Presets

Use these defaults when channel context is known.

## Campaign KV

- Prioritize brand recognition and thematic atmosphere.
- Keep a clear focal subject with headline-safe whitespace.

## Ad Poster

- Prioritize conversion message hierarchy and visual legibility.
- Leave stable text-safe zones for title, offer, and CTA.

## Ecommerce Hero / Main Image

- Prioritize product clarity and shape integrity.
- Avoid decorative background noise that competes with the product.

## Social Cover

- Prioritize thumb-stopping first glance and readable focal contrast.
- Keep layout stable for feed cropping and overlay UI elements.
