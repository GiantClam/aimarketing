# Ratio Presets

Use business context first, then ratio.

| Usage preset | Label | Orientation | Ratio | Typical use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `website_banner` | Website banner 16:9 | landscape | 16:9 | Homepage hero, campaign page masthead |
| `social_cover` | Social cover 4:5 | portrait | 4:5 | Social feed cover, campaign card |
| `ad_poster` | Ad poster 4:3 | landscape | 4:3 | Promotion poster, paid ad creative |
| `avatar` | Avatar image 1:1 | portrait | 1:1 | Profile, icon, square brand mark |

Rules:

- Ask for usage before asking for bare numeric ratio when usage is unknown.
- If usage and numeric ratio both exist, keep user ratio and flag potential channel mismatch.
- For avatar-style tasks, default to 1:1 and avoid extra orientation loops.
