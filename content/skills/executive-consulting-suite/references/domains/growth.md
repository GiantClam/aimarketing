## Role

This domain handles executive-level diagnosis of growth logic, acquisition strategy, channel mix, and demand-generation quality.

Use this domain when the business has an upstream growth-system problem rather than a pure brand interpretation issue or downstream sales execution issue.

## Trigger signals

Use this domain when:
- growth has slowed and the company does not know why
- channel mix appears misaligned with buying motion
- lead flow exists but intent quality is weak
- growth activity is high but commercial outcomes stay noisy
- the company lacks a coherent growth thesis

## Key diagnostic questions

- What is the company’s current growth thesis?
- Which channels should produce demand, and which actually do?
- Is the problem traffic, intent quality, conversion handoff, or mix?
- Are current growth bets economically supportable?
- If sales execution improved tomorrow, would growth still be constrained?

## Common root causes

- **channel mismatch**
- **demand quality weakness**
- **growth thesis ambiguity**
- **acquisition mix drift**
- **handoff weakness between growth and sales**
- **growth investment outrunning economics**

## Evidence discipline

Distinguish:
- **observed**: channel results, inbound quality, conversion by source, CAC or payback signals
- **inferred**: weak growth logic, weak audience fit, poor handoff design
- **hypothesis**: whether growth is primary vs brand or sales-strategy
- **needs data**: by-channel quality, segment response, economics by source

## Recommended action types

- define or refine the growth thesis
- narrow channel focus to higher-intent acquisition paths
- separate awareness channels from demand-generation channels
- improve growth-to-sales handoff logic
- re-sequence growth bets when economics cannot support current ambition

## KPI suggestions

- qualified demand by channel
- lead-to-opportunity quality by source
- CAC and payback by channel where available
- acquisition mix concentration
- source-to-revenue conversion quality

## Boundaries with other domains

- Use `brand` as primary when the main problem is market understanding and trust.
- Use `sales-strategy` as primary when buyer targeting or commercial motion is the bigger problem.
- Use `finance-management` as primary when payback or runway constraints dominate the first decision.

## Anti-patterns

Avoid:
- turning growth diagnosis into campaign tactics
- assuming more leads means better growth
- ignoring economics in growth recommendations
- blaming channels when the real issue is positioning or sales design
