## Role

This domain handles executive-level diagnosis of pipeline discipline, forecast reliability, manager inspection, sales cadence, incentive enforcement, and CRM usage discipline.

Use this domain when the sales strategy is not obviously broken but the commercial system cannot execute consistently.

## Trigger signals

Use this domain when:
- pipeline looks full but is unreliable
- forecasts miss repeatedly
- deal stages are performative rather than real
- managers are not enforcing standards
- qualification discipline is inconsistent
- sales activity looks high but output is unstable

## Key diagnostic questions

- Are qualification standards clear and enforced?
- Are stage definitions real?
- Can leadership trust the forecast?
- Are managers inspecting the right behaviors?
- Are incentives, cadence, and CRM discipline aligned?

## Common root causes

- **qualification weakness**
- **pipeline inflation**
- **forecast unreliability**
- **manager inspection weakness**
- **cadence inconsistency**
- **CRM discipline gaps**
- **incentive-system distortion**

## Evidence discipline

Distinguish:
- **observed**: forecast misses, stale deals, stage slippage, uneven manager performance
- **inferred**: weak inspection, poor standards, low discipline
- **hypothesis**: whether sales management is primary vs organization-hr
- **needs data**: stage aging, manager-level variance, forecast vs actuals, hygiene metrics

## Recommended action types

- tighten qualification and stage discipline
- reset forecast methodology
- strengthen manager inspection and coaching cadence
- align incentives and scorecards with desired behavior
- separate management-system weakness from strategy weakness

## KPI suggestions

- forecast accuracy
- stage aging
- stage-to-stage conversion
- stale-opportunity ratio
- manager-level performance variance
- CRM hygiene completion rate

## Boundaries with other domains

- Use `sales-strategy` as primary when buyer fit, motion, or offer logic is the bigger problem.
- Use `organization-hr` as primary when manager quality, authority, and incentives are part of a broader management-system issue.
- Use `finance-management` as primary when sales underperformance is now mainly a cash or margin problem.

## Anti-patterns

Avoid:
- assuming all sales weakness is strategy weakness
- prescribing tooling changes before diagnosing discipline
- confusing pipeline volume with pipeline quality
- treating coaching problems as purely individual-rep problems
