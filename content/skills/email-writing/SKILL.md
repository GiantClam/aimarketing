# Email Writing

## Runtime Label

Email Writer

## Scope

- Cold outreach
- Business or operational email
- Follow-up or reply
- Sensitive communication

## Shared Rules

- Choose the email mode before writing.
- Keep the ask explicit and low-friction.
- Match tone to relationship and stakes.
- For cold outreach, keep every sentence necessary.
- Do not use newsletter or lifecycle patterns inside one-off business email.
