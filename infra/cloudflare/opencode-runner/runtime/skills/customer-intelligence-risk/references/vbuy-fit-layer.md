## Core positioning

VBUY is a B2B customized textile supply chain partner, not a pure trading company and not a single-category towel factory.

Use these positioning anchors:
- B2B customized textile supplier
- home textiles, beach towels, microfiber towels, sports towels, cotton towels
- end-to-end capability from fiber or fabric to finished products
- integrated trade and manufacturing model
- three-location coordination across Shenzhen, Jiangyin, and Anhui
- customization, quality control, delivery certainty, compliance cooperation, and long-term stable business relationships

Do not claim unverified revenue, employee count, equipment count, monthly capacity, patents, global ranking, or fixed customer endorsements.

- MOQ depends on material, process, printing, packaging, and order structure
- order confirmation, pre-production sample, progress feedback, pre-shipment confirmation, optional third-party inspection

Do not promise a fixed MOQ, fixed lead time, or fixed capacity without order-specific confirmation.

## Compliance prompts

Use compliance prompts as BD confirmation items, not as legal advice.

## Risk interpretation for VBUY

In addition to the default risk categories, consider:
- price pressure from heavy discounting or low-price positioning
- refund, shipping, or customer-service complaints that may signal operational instability
- unclear purchasing or contracting entity
- unclear authorization, licensing, collaboration, or territory boundaries
- high customization demands without clear approval process
- children's product, California, EU, or sustainability claims requiring extra testing
- small batch expectations that may not fit the requested process or price point

Do not turn consumer complaints into legal conclusions. Label them as customer experience or operational signals.

## Message guardrails

Do not use these claims unless specifically verified for the exact product and order:
- 100% antibacterial
- never fades
- no marks at all
- strongest, first, top global, industry-leading without evidence
- medical use or protective function
- organic cotton
- biodegradable
- patented
- same factory as a famous brand
- certified for every order or every market

Safer wording:
- can cooperate with required testing
- certification or audit cooperation may be discussed by product and order
- high color-fastness direction when supported by testing
- not easy to leave marks, rather than no marks at all
- recycled material direction when material and certification scope are confirmed
